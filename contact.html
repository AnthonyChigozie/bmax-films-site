Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for weddings and major events. However, we can often accommodate shorter notice depending on availability. Contact us to check our schedule.

What's included in your packages?

Each package varies by service type, but generally includes pre-event consultation, professional filming with high-end equipment, professional editing, color grading, music licensing, and digital delivery. Specific details are outlined on our services page.

How long does editing take?

Editing timelines vary by project complexity. Wedding films typically take 4-6 weeks, while shorter projects like real estate videos are usually completed within 1-2 weeks. Rush delivery options are available for an additional fee.

Do you travel for events?

Yes! We serve clients across Abuja (FCT) and are available for events anywhere in Nigeria. Travel fees may apply for locations outside our standard service area. We love capturing stories in unique places across the country.

What equipment do you use?

We use professional cinema cameras, stabilizers, drones, and audio equipment. Our gear includes the latest technology to ensure stunning 4K and even 6K footage with cinematic quality.

How many videographers will be at my event?

This depends on your package and event size. Typically, we provide 1-3 videographers to ensure comprehensive coverage from multiple angles. We'll discuss the best option during consultation.
